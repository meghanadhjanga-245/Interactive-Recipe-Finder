About Interactive Recipe Finder

Interactive Recipe Finder is a smart pantry-based recipe recommendation tool. You just type in the ingredients available at your home, and our system finds matching recipes using the Spoonacular API.

  • Enter your ingredients in the search bar.
  • Click “Find Recipes” to get suggestions.
  • Browse results with cooking time & servings.
  • Click a recipe to view full details and ingredients.
  • Rate recipes ⭐ after exploring.

Demo Walkthrough

  1. Go to the home page.
  2. Enter the ingredients you have (e.g., “eggs, tomato, basil”).
  3. Click “Find Recipes.”
  4. Browse through the recipes shown in a grid layout.
  5. Click “View Recipe” to see ingredients, cooking time & servings.
  6. After closing, rate your recipe with stars ⭐.
